# MessageDict
Voice-to-Text iOS Dictation
Generate polished text messages for iMessage, WhatsApp & Co - securely and free.


# What is MessageDict?
MessageDict is an iOS Shortcut that transforms your voice into perfectly formatted text messages. Simply dictate your message, and MessageDict will clean it upâ€”removing stutters, adding proper punctuation, and formatting it correctly. It's what Apple's dictation should have been.


# Features:

- Clean, properly formatted messages from voice input
- Removes verbal fillers and stutters
- Adds correct punctuation automatically
- Works in English and German
- Completely free and privacy-focused

## Requirements

Before installing MessageDict, you'll need:

1. **iOS Shortcuts app** (pre-installed on iOS 13+)
2. **Claude for iOS app** (free download)

## Installation

### Step 1: Install iOS Shortcuts

The Shortcuts app comes pre-installed on iOS 13 and later. If you've removed it, reinstall from the App Store.

**Download Shortcuts:** https://apps.apple.com/de/app/shortcuts/id1462947752

### Step 2: Install Claude for iOS

Download the Claude app and complete the setup process. The app is free to use.

**Download Claude:** https://apps.apple.com/de/app/claude-by-anthropic/id6473753684

## Privacy & Security

All processing happens on your device except for the Claude AI transcription service. Your voice recordings and messages are not stored permanently. Contact information remains on your device.
